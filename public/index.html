About Us

Presagia operationalizes advances in statistical/machine learning beyond the academic domain, into real applications in drug development. Our emphasis is strategic planning and trials with simple designs that have been optimized based on existing evidence.

Precision medicine as an endeavor resides at the intersections of machine learning, clinical trial design, biology, and medicine. Contemporary drug develop programs require a toolbox that extends beyond conventional statistical analysis and designs. Presagia Inc. provides strategic guidance in the use of predictive analytics in drug development. Our solutions have been devised, studied, tested and published in academic journals.
