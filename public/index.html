About Us

Presagia operationalizes advances in statistical/machine learning beyond the academic domain, into real applications in drug development. Our emphasis is strategic planning and trials with simple designs that have been optimized based on existing evidence.

With the emergence of tumor agnostic therapies and the evolution of the regulatory landscape (notably FDASIA), the breakthrough therapy designation bring new focus on single-cohort analyses of subsets of large early phase trials. These trials have been defined by inclusive eligibility criteria and extensive enrollment into expasion cohorts. Multi-indication drug development strategies have resulted. These strategies emphasize breakthrough designation in primary indication with rapid expansion to additional indications. Our group provides strategic guidance in this procedure through the use of analytics that interrogate several aspects of accruing trial data. The guidance we provide is based on analytical solutions that have been devised, studied, and published in academia. Our goal is to operationalize these methods in order to more quickly evaluate therapies and quickly bring effective thrapies to market. We have experience leveraging the evolving public trial databases to identify and quantify uncertainties in drug development and establish syntetic controls. Additionally we evaluate subpopulation treatment benefit heterogeneity following studies.
